What is Kali Linux?

Kali Linux is a Debian-based Linux distribution aimed at advanced penetration testing and security auditing.

Why use Kali Linux?

Kali Linux comes with a large number of pre-installed tools that make it easy to perform various security testing tasks. It is also highly customizable and can be configured to meet specific needs.
